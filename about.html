About Us

LE SHARK (INDIA) is a 30 year old Manufacturer and Exporter of readymade Garments. Its manufacturing & finishing plants are spread over well layed out and well equipped Factories spread over 2,00,000 sq. ft. in South India. The owners belongs to a family that has been in the clothing industry for the last 40 years.Both Le shark (India) and Hyknit Industries operate jointly in Manufacturing and Marketing Both these companies are well known for Quality, Innovation, Response to the customer queries, Product Development & Best Price For Quality etc... Both companies are Government recognised Export Houses and fit in the slot of "Most Dynamic Company" on the knit scene in India.
