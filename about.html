Who We Are

Empire Clothing is a MSME certified garment manufacturing company based out of Tirupur which is one of the India’s leading garment manufacturing hubs. We commenced operations from 2017 and our well-equipped production unit spread across 3000 sq. feet with a production capacity of 25000 pieces per month. With our contemporary machinery and skilled work force, we have been catering to the renowned brands across the globe.

Our Mission

We have a passionate team who always strides towards the best-in-class quality and timely delivery for all our customers. We strengthen our capabilities year after year through teamwork and the passion to excel. We as an enterprise always committed to the satisfaction of our customers.

Our Vision

To be the most innovative, sustainable and socially responsible garment manufacturer through our unique approach to customer service, manufacturing excellence and employee engagement.
